About Us... Committed to Quality!

Artisan Renovations is a fully insured remodeling company that has served communities in Houston and surrounding areas for nearly three decades. Customer service and satisfaction is our top priority. Our promise is to deliver top quality service and value to our customers in a highly professional manner at a competitive price.

When you hire Artisan Renovations for any remodeling or custom woodwork project, you can expect a highly professional team who work together to produce a well-crafted product and a highly-satisfied customer. Our designers, electricians, plumbers, plasterers, painters and other subcontractors are all top quality professionals as well. Because of this work ethic, and because our objective is to give our customers 100% satisfaction, much of our work is for repeat customers, and a large percentage of our requests for new work comes from referrals.

Stephen Schertz

Founder, Owner & CEO

Artisan Renovations is a design-build firm founded in 1994 by Stephen Schertz. Specializing in residential and commercial remodeling, we have an outstanding work ethic and a respected business that encompasses all forms of woodworking. We take pride in our work and our meticulous efforts are evident in custom cabinets and furniture produced by the Artisan Renovations team. We are a team of highly skilled craftsman who strive for perfection and are constantly learning more about the trade. When you request work from Artisan Renovations, we ensure your project is estimated and bid accurately and competitively. If your project requires custom woodwork, we employ master cabinetmakers that can undertake even the most complex woodworking projects. For general contracts and remodeling projects, Artisan Renovations offers design services, order and procurement of materials and scheduling of jobs and subcontractors. We oversee all cabinet fabrication and installation and guarantee the utmost quality in the finished product.

Design/Build Process

Attention to detail from start to finish

Our Design/Build Approach

Our design/build approach to building brings together professional design and construction expertise. This joining of the design and construction functions also can save you time and money, and helps make you — the customer — more of a partner in the construction or remodeling of your home or business. Our process is unique, because it is flexible. We can work with your architect's drawing, or Artisan Renovations is fully capabile of producing architectural quality drawngs as well as providing a photo-realistic 3D rendering of your project. Artisan Renovations offers you more than just gorgeous cabinetry. From the start, design solutions are strongly rooted in practical application and experience. Project planning takes into account the realities of the construction process and the budget you have established. The result is a remodeled home or custom cabinetry that suits your special needs and preferences. Whether you're in the market to remodel a room, or create an addition for your existing home, working with Artisan Renovations' unique design/build process may be just right for you.

Design Build Process

Let us design a professional 3D rendering of your project BEFORE you build. Any style or color changes are only a mouse-click away an the original design can be updated quickly.

Advantages of Our Approach

From your initial phone call to Artisan Renovations through the design and construction phases, you are a member of the team. As a team, we decide how the process works best for your project and we work together each step of the way as your home is transformed. We strive to become your "contractor for life" with outstanding service and impeccable construction.

If your project involves custom woodwork or cabinetry, we will work with you to develop a functional design - allowing you to work hand-in-hand with one company from start to finish, design through construction and installation.

We strive to ensure every detail in your design is included in your project price, or a budget allowance has been made if you have not yet selected your finish materials. Owner-provided items are indicated in the contract.

We believe you will find our Design/Build process has many advantages and benefits for you.

 

Construction Budget

Often homeowners want to establish their project costs before they are certain of the changes they want for their homes. Stated desires such as "replace my kitchen" or "update my bathroom" do not provide us enough information to determine a remodeling budget. Additional square footage may or may not solve your design dilemma, and each home has its own structural and site situations that require resolution during the planning stage of design. Artisan Renovations considers the structural, financial and design elements simultaneously. The value of various aspects of the design has to be determined by the homeowner, the architect (if necessary) and Artisan Renovations, as the design develops.

Choosing from your wish list and working within your budget are often the most challenging parts of design development. We provide guidance and, if needed, we can assist you with modifying the project into various phases of remodeling, or reduce the scope of work to align your remodeling needs and desires with your budget.
